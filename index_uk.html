Doctrine

  • Bible
    • Bible We believe, that the Bible consist of 39 canonical books of Old Testament and of 27 canonical books of New Testament. That it's the Word of God and as a Holy Scripture is inspired by God, infallible and is full revelation and the highest authority in matters of faith and christian life.
  • God
    • We believe in one God, Creator of the Universe, Everlasting and Allmighty, as our Father, which is in heaven.
  • Son of God
    • We believe in divinity of Jesus Christ - One and only Son of God, Savior and mediator, born out of flesh from virgin Mary, - in His surrogate death on the cross for the sins of the world, - In His resurection in flesh and ascension, - In His second comming.
  • Holy Spirit
    • We believe, that the Holy Spirit is a godly person originated out of Father and the Son, everlasting with Father and the Son. He was given to the Church as a spokesman , who lives in Church and in each believers and that he gives His gifts to the newborn man, according to His will.
  • Last days and eternity
    • We believe in: - resurection of flesh - rapture of believers - Second comming of Christ on earth and establishing by Him Millenia Kingdom - Everlasting salvation and everlasting damnation.
  • Baptism of faith
    • Wierzymy, że jest wolą Pana Jezusa Chrystusa, aby odrodzony chrześcijanin, który stał się Jego naśladowcą, został w wieku świadomym ochrzczony przez zanurzenie w wodzie. We believe, that it's the will of Lord Jesus Christ, that newly born christian, who has become His follower, should be deliberately baptized by immersion in water.
  • Lord's supper
    • Wierzymy, że jest wolą Pana Jezusa Chrystusa, aby obchodzić pamiątkę śmierci Pańskiej pod dwiema postaciami: chleba i wina. We believe, that it is the will of Lord Jesus Christ, to commemorate the Lord's death in two forms: bread and wine.
  • The unity of Church
    • Wierzymy, że wszyscy odrodzeni, wierzący w Pana Jezusa Chrystusa są członkami Kościoła, którego głową jest Chrystus. Każdy, kto przyjął przez wiarę Pana Jezusa Chrystusa, przyjął równocześnie Ojca i Ducha Świętego. Każdy nowonarodzony chrześcijanin ma Ducha Świętego jako zadatek i powinien dążyć do życia w Jego pełni. We believe, that all born, believing in Lord JEsus Christ are members of a Church, which head is Christ. Everyone, who's accepted thrugh faith Lord Jesus Christ, has also accepted Father and the Holy Ghost. Every new born christian has a Holy Ghost as a down payment and he should strive to live in His fullness.
  • Rules of life
    • Every believer observes the principle of universal priesthood and belongs to one of the churches. Members live according to the commandment: - to love God and neighbour and - they continue in apostolic teaching, in communion with believers, in breaking bread and in prayers.
  • Attitude to the state
    • Church members, according to the New Testament teachings, are loyal citizens and strive for the prosperity of their earthly homeland.
